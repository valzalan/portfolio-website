Resumé

Who am I?

Zalán Valkó

years old Computer Science student based in Miskolc, Hungary.

My fascination with computers started at an early age, I was stunned by movie visual effects. I desperately wanted to learn the craft, so I started reading about design.

Few years down the line I've gained a lot of experience editing photos, and making various logos and designs with Photoshop. I also started tinkering with 3D modelling and advanced to a not so bad level at it. As I was finishing high school, and started looking for University courses I realized what I was really interested in. And although I enjoyed designing, software and web development really fascinated me more. So I applied for a CS degree.

And after that I just slipped down a deep rabbit hole. I feel like I want to know every possible thing about computers and development, every technology that is available or will be. But what especially intrigues me is machine learning and web application development. I wish to further my knowledge in these areas in the future.
